Sony’s PlayMemories Camera Apps (PMCA) platform allowed users to install specialized Android-based applications directly onto their cameras to expand their hardware's capabilities.

As of August 31, 2025, Sony has officially terminated the download service for all apps (both paid and free). While they can no longer be officially downloaded, many users with compatible cameras (like your a6000) still use them or look for archived versions via community tools like PMCA-RE.

App Name,Functionality
